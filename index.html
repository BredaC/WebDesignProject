Bundoran

Bundoran is proud to be the first Donegal stop on the Wild Atlantic Way’s Surf Coast. Stand at the Tullan Strand Discovery Point and take in Donegal Bay with views of Slieve League, Mullaghmore, Benbulben and the picturesque Dartry Mountains.
In Bundoran, you’ll find so much to do from outdoor activities to live music to the best in food and drink. Whether you’re travelling with your family, you’re a couple or just on your own and looking for a fun or relaxing few days away.
