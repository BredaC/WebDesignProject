Surfing

Bundoran is known as Ireland’s Surfing Capital and any day throughout the year you can find locals and visitors taking advantage of the most wonderful of natural amenities right on our doorstep – the Atlantic ocean. From beginner to pro, there’s always a wave in the area with 5 surf schools providing expert tuition, advice, board and wetsuit hire.

Tullan Strand

Tullan Strand, is considered one of the best surf beaches in Ireland and is a Wild Atlantic Way Discovery Point. It stretches for 2 kilometres with sand dunes and an impressive backdrop of the Dartry Mountains. It is considered by many to be one of Ireland’s most picturesque beaches.

The Peak

One of the most famous surf spots in Ireland, The Peak is located at the southern end of Bundoran’s main beach. It’s a powerful wave that’s suitable for experienced surfers and can get crowded during peak season.
